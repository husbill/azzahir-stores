Home / About
Our story

Stocking the staples that taste like home

Azzahir Stores started with one frustration: finding rice that actually holds up in a proper pot of jollof, and seasonings that taste like the ones back home. We searched the markets, tested the mills, and built relationships directly with growers and trusted Nigerian brands so our customers wouldn't have to compromise.

Today we serve households and bulk buyers across Nigeria and the diaspora — from a young family in Lagos to a grandmother in Houston who hasn't found Ofada rice this good anywhere else. Every bag, tin and bottle we sell is something we'd put on our own table.

Our mission is simple: premium African and Nigerian staples, sourced honestly, delivered fast.

Home / Returns & Refunds

Returns & Refunds

Eligibility

Unopened, unexpired packaged goods can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Perishable and fresh/frozen items are final sale once delivered, unless damaged in transit.

Damaged or incorrect items

Send a photo via WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery and we'll arrange a free replacement or full refund — no return shipping required.

Refund timing

Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
